PORTFOLIO

INCUB8

SUMMER 2018 - PRESENT

INCUB8 is a web app that allows teen start-ups to share their projects with potential investors and obtain the funding and expertise necessary to develop their ideas. It was initially created during the SF Kode with Klossy program. Responsibilities included the majority of front-end and back-end coding, using Python Flask and mongoDB. The app was recently submitted it to the Congressional App Challenge.

UHS Baking Club

OCTOBER 2018

Developed a website to publicize the club, sign up members, and to serve as a platform to share recipes and ideas. The Baking Club at San Francisco University High School has a primary goal of helping students to de-stress in a stressful environment by sharing recipes, watching baking TV shows, and eating some *good* baked goods.

DiaTribe College Day

JULY 2018

Created a website to advertise the annual college day event held by the diaTribe Foundation. Developed a sign up system for interested high school students, set up a variety of social media accounts and publicized the event. Also set up a live broadcast of the event on the diaTribe Facebook page.

MailChimp Aggregator

AUGUST 2018

Assisted in the creation of an aggregator that pulled data from the MailChimp API to summarize click open rates. Using data analyzed success factors in most read articles to and help make newsletters sent by diaTribe more efficient.

SF SPCA Foster Website

APRIL 2016

Created a website for foster parents at a local animal shelter, the San Francisco SPCA, to be able to share photos and information. Foster parents could send photos to an email address which would then automatically be added to the database and go out to major social media channels.
